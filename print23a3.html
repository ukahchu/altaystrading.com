About Us

 

Introduction

Specialized in the supply and installation of natural & reconstituted stone and mosaics, Al Hashem Marble has established itself as a leader in the UAE market for the past 40 years. Our operations have recently expanded to handle large-scale projects in other GCC countries, and the USA through our subdivision TerraViva- AHTV. 

  • Our experienced & innovative people, equipped with industry's top technology, help our clients bring their ideas to reality. 
  • An ISO 9001:2008 certified company, AHMC is committed to deliver quality and value to our clients.

 

Vision 

Partnership with our innovative people and inspired clients is key. Building on a long-term reputation for quality and commitment; we strive to reach and sustain a future as a leader in the marble and stone industry in the UAE, GCC and the world. 

 

Mission

At AHMC, our experienced & innovative people help our clients bring their designs and stone ideas to reality. We translate marble and stone to solid long-lasting value to our clients. Whether for residential or commercial projects, we are always on the look to contribute to the prosperity of the UAE and the world.
